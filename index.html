Chaitanya Vigyanam Science Festival of the PSBB Group of Schools. It is a brainchild of our beloved
Mrs.YG Parthasarathy.

The main objectives of the events are
> To develop 21st century skills with a focus on scientific knowledge, creativity, critical thinking, problem solving and team work.
> To develop designing abilities, to interlink science and art, and to encourage a multidisciplinary approach
> To create the next generation of scientists and engineers, to celebrate and inspire curiosity and
> To empower students to engage in their profession of choice later on , with confidence.
